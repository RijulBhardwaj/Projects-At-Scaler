## ❓ **Business Questions to Answer**

### **📈 Acquisition Analysis**

* Identify months with the **highest and lowest customer acquisition**.
* Analyze patterns to determine months that consistently perform better or worse.
* Recommend strategies to **stabilize acquisition growth** year-round.

### **🔄 Retention Analysis**

* Identify months with **strongest and weakest retention rates**.
* Study customer behavior during high-retention periods and replicate successful patterns.
* Recommend retention strategies for weaker months.

### **💰 Revenue & Customer Type Comparison**

* Compare MoM revenue from **new vs. existing customers**.
* Analyze trends to balance acquisition and retention focus.

### **🏷️ Coupon & Discount Effectiveness**

* Study the relationship between **coupon usage and revenue**.
* Recommend optimized discount strategies that maximize revenue while keeping profitability intact.

### **🛒 Product Performance**

* Identify **top-performing products** and analyze factors driving their success.
* Use insights to guide inventory decisions and promotional planning.

### **📢 Marketing Efficiency**

* Analyze relationship between **monthly marketing spend vs. revenue**.
* Identify months with unusually high/low ROI.
* Suggest ways to improve marketing efficiency and budget allocation.

### **👥 Customer Segmentation (RFM)**

* Segment customers into **Premium, Gold, Silver, Standard**.
* Recommend targeted strategies for each segment.
* Analyze revenue contribution of each segment to prioritize business focus.

### **📆 Cohort & Lifetime Value Analysis**

* Group customers by their **month of first purchase** and study retention over time.
* Evaluate **LTV by acquisition month** to inform strategy.

### **🏷️ Coupon Impact (Statistical Test)**

* Compare **average transaction values** of coupon vs. non-coupon users.
* Conduct a statistical test to validate differences.

### **👤 Demographic & Pricing Behavior Analysis**

* Analyze differences in purchase behavior across:

  * Locations
  * Age groups
  * Delivery charge tiers
* Use insights to build **personalized marketing and pricing strategies**.

### **⏳ Customer Tenure Analysis**

* Analyze whether **customer tenure impacts purchase frequency**.
* Provide strategies to increase the engagement of long-term customers.

### **🚚 Delivery Charges & Order Behavior**

* Study the effect of **delivery charges on order frequency and order value**.
* Suggest optimized delivery pricing strategies.

### **💸 Tax & Delivery Fee Impact**

* Evaluate how **taxes and delivery fees** influence customer spending.

### **📆 Seasonal & Daily Sales Trends**

* Identify **seasonal trends** by category and location.
* Analyze **daily sales patterns** to identify high- and low-performing days.
* Suggest strategies to boost sales during low-performance periods.
