# ❓ **Business Questions / Tasks**

## 1️⃣ **Data Preprocessing & Feature Engineering**

* Clean, prepare, and explore the IPL dataset.
* Engineer features capturing match dynamics (venue impact, toss effect, margin metrics, team strength indicators, etc.)

---

## 2️⃣ **Text Analytics — Player Performance**

* Use NLP techniques on *Player of the Match* descriptions.
* Identify high-impact performance patterns across seasons and venues.

---

## 3️⃣ **Team Performance Clustering**

* Cluster teams using performance metrics (win %, run rate, wicket ratios, consistency scores).
* Identify teams with similar strengths & weaknesses.

---

## 4️⃣ **Strategic Pattern Mining**

* Apply association rule mining to discover “winning combinations” such as:

  * Toss decision + venue
  * Batting order + pitch type
  * Team composition patterns tied to wins

---

## 5️⃣ **Match Outcome Prediction — Logistic Regression**

* Build a logistic regression model to predict the match winner.
* Focus on feature selection, regularization, interpretability, ROC/AUC.

---

## 6️⃣ **Venue Recommendation System**

* Build a **content-based recommendation system** suggesting favorable venues for each team.
* Optional: Collaborative filtering based on historical match performance.

---

## 7️⃣ **Performance Trend Analysis**

* Analyze decade-long team performance trends.
* Use modeling techniques to **forecast future outcomes**.

---

## 8️⃣ **Player of the Match Prediction — KNN**

* Use KNN to predict POTM using match context + historical performance similarities.

---

## 9️⃣ **Toss Decision Strategy — Decision Tree**

* Build a decision tree to recommend whether to **bat or bowl** after winning the toss.
* Extract interpretable rules for captains.

---

## 🔟 **Advanced Match Prediction — Ensemble Models**

* Compare:

  * Bagging
  * Boosting
* Evaluate which ensemble performs best for match outcome prediction.

---

## ⭐ Bonus: **Fan Sentiment Impact Analysis**

* Use sentiment analysis of social media/fan commentary.
* Analyze connection between public sentiment and team performance.
